## What

"A human probably wrote this" = a spaced display name (`First Last`) AND an address that does not look automated (`no-reply`, `notifications@`, `billing@`, …) AND no big-brand token in address or name.

## Origin

The difference between archiving a receipt and archiving your accountant.
